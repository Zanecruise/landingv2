We Don't Just Analyze. We Deliver a Verdict.

Introducing Reputation-as-a-Transaction (RaaT). An execution layer that conditions every transaction on the parties' reputation, in real-time.

1

Transaction Input

Every transaction, whether Pix, wire transfer, or a digital asset operation, is received by our API as a data point to be validated.

2

Reputational Analysis

The Spezzatura engine analyzes the transaction in real-time, cross-referencing hundreds of on-chain and off-chain variables to generate a deterministic risk score.

3

Verdict Delivery

A final verdict (ALLOW, REVIEW, BLOCK) is delivered via API, allowing your systems to execute the appropriate action in an automated and auditable way.
