The Spezzatura Oracle is a deterministic and auditable reputational mechanism, designed to validate trust decisions in Web3 and TradFi environments.

Its purpose is to validate decisions with reproducible logic. Each decision generates a numerical score, a verdict (ALLOW, REVIEW, BLOCK), and a textual rationale with a SHA-256 hash.
