Umbrella

Portfolio analysis for investment advisors.

Umbrella is a platform developed for investment advisory firms. Through it, the advisor can perform a complete and detailed analysis of their clients' portfolios, asset by asset. The platform cross-references portfolio data with the current macroeconomic scenario, generating scores, risk flags, and valuable insights to optimize decision-making and wealth management.

Key Features

  • Complete Portfolio Analysis: Detailed evaluation of each asset in the client's portfolio, identifying risks and opportunities.
  • Integration with Macroeconomic Scenario: Compare the portfolio's composition with current economic trends and projections.
  • Risk Scores and Flags: Assignment of risk scores and automatic alerts for assets or strategies that require attention.
  • Portfolio Optimization: Data-driven suggestions for rebalancing and asset allocation, aiming to maximize returns and minimize risks.
  • Customized Client Reports: Generate clear and professional reports to communicate the analysis and recommendations to your clients.
